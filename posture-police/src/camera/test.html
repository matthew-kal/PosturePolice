POSTURE POLICE

Maintain Perfect Posture

Your personal posture monitoring system

The Posture Police application monitors your sitting position and alerts you in real time when your posture needs correction. To get started:

  1. Click the "Start Posture Monitoring" button in the main view
  2. Adjust your monitoring settings in the Settings tab
  3. Receive alerts when your posture needs correction
  4. Stay healthy with proper ergonomic positioning

Note: Your camera feed is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. All posture analysis is performed entirely on your device to protect your privacy and support your well-being.

Monitoring Settings

Enables or disables camera. When active, your camera will be used to analyze your posture.
1.0
Adjusts how sensitive the posture detection is. Higher values will detect smaller posture changes, while lower values are more forgiving.
3s
Sets how long the system waits before alerting you about poor posture. A longer delay prevents alerts for momentary posture changes.
50%
Controls the volume of alert sounds. Set to 0% to have visual alerts only without sound.
